About Traffic Tom

Traffic Tom is an engaging traffic management simulation game that puts you in control of busy intersections. Your mission is to prevent accidents and ensure smooth traffic flow by managing vehicles, pedestrians, and traffic signals effectively.

Game Features

  • Intuitive traffic control mechanics
  • Multiple challenging levels
  • Progressive difficulty system
  • Real-time traffic management
  • Score-based progression

How to Play

Control traffic lights and guide vehicles safely through intersections. Keep an eye on the traffic flow, prevent accidents, and maintain efficiency to achieve high scores. The better you perform, the more challenging the scenarios become!
